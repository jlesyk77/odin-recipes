Burger Sauce

First you need to make the burger sauce which is incredibly easy!

Just mix the following list of ingredients together in the ratios listed and you'll have a delicious sauce to lather your burgers with!

  • 1 part Ketchup
  • 3/4 part Mayo
  • 1/4 part Mustard
  • 1/4 part Hot Sauce of choice
  • 1/4 part Worchestshire Sauce

The (Smash)burgers

Burger Ingredients:
  • 80% lean/ 20% fat ground beef
  • Brioche buns
  • American Cheese slices
  • Salt and Pepper

The next thing to do is to make your patties.

It'll be easy to source the beef because you just need to use a 80/20 lean-fat ground beef.

The patties should be made in 2.5 oz balls.

Fire up a flat top grill as high as it will go and adequately grease it.

Then follow the rest of the directions:

  1. For each burger use 2 patties.
  2. Place both patties on the hot flat-top and press them down flat.
  3. Sprinkle with Salt and Pepper
  4. After 1-2 minutes, as soon as the patty is fully browned on the bottom, flip each.
  5. Immediately put 1 slice of American Cheese on each patty.
  6. Place both patties on your brioche bun.
  7. ENJOY!
